What Makes Me, Me

My preferred genre is all types of metal, however it's not the only genre I listen to. I love music that has great flow. For the past few months I've been working with this local metal band that is just as passionate as making music as I am.

Books
When I first moved out of my parents, I was luck enough to meet my best friend who later lent me "Star Wars: Darth Bane Path of Destruction" which taught me that knowledge is power. Since then books have become a huge part in what makes me, me. Imagination and passion are things I cherish deeply, becuase I feel that without these, life seems less colorful and dull.
