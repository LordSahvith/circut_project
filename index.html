What Makes Me, Me

Music

No way I can even go a whole day without it. I have been playing guitar since I was 13 and use it as my meditation at the end the day. My preferred genre is all types of metal, however it's not the only genre I listen to. I love music that has great flow. For the past few months I've been working with this local metal band that is just as passionate as making music as I am.

Games

Video games have been a part of my life since I was very young. My dad owned a few arcades during my childhood, so naturally I haven't grown out playing when I can. My favorite type of video game are RPGs, mainly because I love adventure, action and above all great stories. My love for games is the biggest reason that I got into coding. In my past times I like to make classic arcade games with the Phaser framework that uses JavaScript.

Coding

My love for video games as mentioned above is one of my biggest influences next to my oldest brother whose been coding for 20+ years. When I was in high school I moved to new school who had a web development class and since I had a free period I took it not knowing what to expect. The idea that some written words of magic that the computer seemed to understand and tranform into a work of art was something that I instantly fell in love with. It wasn't until I got to Salt Lake Community College did I learn more about the web and the languages that make it work and seem to have become addicted to learning more!

Books

During grade school I never appreciated what a book could really do for you. It wasn't until I moved out of my parents and was learning that life is much different on your own. Luckily I had made an amazing friend that lent me "Star Wars: Darth Bane Path of Destruction", which completely changed my life honestly. This book taught me that knowledge is power and you should never stop seeking both knowledge and understanding. Since then books have become a huge part in what makes me, me. Imagination and passion are things I cherish deeply, becuase I feel that without those life seems less colorful.
